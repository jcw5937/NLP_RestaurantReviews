Bug Reflection Evaluator

Final project tool — evaluate your self-reflections across 10 intentional bug cases

How to use this tool:
  1. Fill in the four fields and click Generate prompt.
  2. Copy the prompt, paste it into Claude.ai, and copy the response back.
  3. Paste the response and select Good or Bad.
  4. If Bad — a new reflection box appears. Revise your thinking, generate a new prompt, and repeat until you get Good.
